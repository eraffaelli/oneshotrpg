One Shot RPG

Short Sci-Fi and Horror RPG Adventures

One Shot RPG is a line of short role playing game adventures set in a sci-fi and horror setting. Players choose from premade characters to confront the monster of the week. Each adventure is playable from start to finish in a single game night. The setting, characters, and first adventure are currently in development.
